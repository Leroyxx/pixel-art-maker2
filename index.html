Instructions

  1. Supply the width, height and size of every square side for your grid and hit "Make Grid!" to create your dream canvas of squares.
  2. Choose a color to draw with from the palette (left click it) or pick a custom color. Right click a square on the palette to change eraser's color.
  3. Click (and continue to drag if you wish) the mouse over the squares in the canvas in order to draw.
  4. Erase by right clicking (and continuing to drag, again, if you wish) with your mouse.
  5. Hold CTRL and left or right click a square on the palette to store your pen/eraser color in it correspondingly.
  6. Clicking one of the two tool icons will equip them. Use the Paint Bucket tool to contaminate an area with an ever-spreading color until it meets its limits.
  7. Or you can hold CTRL and press the Paint Bucket to fill the pixel canvas entirely.
  8. Your canvas of squares will be minimized and displayed on the bottom left.
  9. If you wish to undo, press CTRL and Z. CTRL + SHIFT + Z will result in a redo.
